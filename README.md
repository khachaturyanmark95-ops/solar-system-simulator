# Solar System Orbital Simulator

An animated simulation of the Solar System using Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation.

## Physics
- Newton's Law of Gravity: F = G * M * m / r²
- Real orbital velocities and distances from NASA data
- Gravitational acceleration calculated at every time step

## Why 4 planets?
Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars were chosen because they fit on one screen with a clear view of their orbits. The outer planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) are too far away — showing all 8 planets would make the inner planets invisible.

## Technologies
- Python 3
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

## Planets
  - Mercury: 57.9 million km, 47,400 m/s
- Venus: 108.2 million km, 35,020 m/s
- Earth: 149.6 million km, 29,780 m/s
- Mars: 227.9 million km, 24,130 m/s
